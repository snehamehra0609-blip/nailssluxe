How to Measure at Home

  1. Place a piece of transparent tape across the widest part of your natural nail bed.
  2. Mark the edges of your nail walls with a fine pen.
  3. Remove the tape and place it against a ruler to measure the distance in millimeters.
  4. Repeat for all fingers on both left and right hands.

Tip: If you fall between sizes, we recommend sizing UP. You can gently file down the edges of the press-ons for an exact fit.
